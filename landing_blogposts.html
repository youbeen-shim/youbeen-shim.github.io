Landing: Blogposts

This page is probably the most frequently updated, and contents a wide verity of posts (as expected of a blog, I suppose) that can include: some minor topics of discussion that I find relevant, projects that are self-contained or otherwise not as monumental, topics that I've organized to review later on, and break-down of some of my major projects to show progress and hopefully provide guidance if someone wished to follow along. There will also be sprinkles of my life here and there, this is Scraps of Random Brain Activity, after all, and consistency is not guaranteed.

If you see an error somewhere that you'd like to point out, I'd gladly look through it and edit it. Otherwise, please note that I am not an ultimate authority on the things that I'm working on. I will, however, do my best to explain my decisions and assumptions where appropriate and include sources that inspired me so that others can have multiple reference points on where to start.
